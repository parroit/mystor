You need an online file storage site for your customers. Mystor make it easy to do it.

The idea is simple: you have to get an ftp account on a ftp server. You don't need one with multiple configurable accounts. Just a plain, flat account with some space available.
Than, you setup a Mystor server and link it to the ftp server.
Create your customer accounts, and you are ready to share files with them!
